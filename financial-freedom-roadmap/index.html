The Financial Freedom Roadmap

A structured path from control to compounding

It took me several years to fully understand and appreciate this roadmap. I didn’t invent it myself — there are a couple of books that detail similar approaches, both of which are at the top of my Reading List. I’ve adapted it in a way that makes sense to me and built some Calculators that help visualise the power of executing against these steps.

Audit → Simplify → Scale → Invest → Track
  1. 1
    Recognise

    Audit

    Freedom starts with control, not investing. Audit the last 12 months of spending to find the leaks in your bucket, then calculate your Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio to see exactly where you stand

  2. 2
    Prioritise & Optimise

    Simplify

    Run every expense through a prioritisation matrix. Cut, downgrade or optimise the non-essentials so more of your income survives to be put to work

  3. 3
    Build the base

    Scale

    Bank a 3–6 month buffer for security, then invest in your highest-ROI asset first—yourself. Self-development and mobile income widen the gap between what you earn and what you spend

  4. 4
    Invest & compound

    Expand investments

    Direct the surplus into genuine, appreciating assets—favour low-cost index funds over speculation. Then reinvest and let compounding do the heavy lifting, steadily increasing your allocation as your assets grow

  5. 5
    Measure your freedom

    Track

    Score your progress with the Freedom Formula. When leveraged income covers your lifestyle, work becomes optional—you’re free

The Freedom Formula

A simple way to measure how close to financial freedom you really are

LI Leveraged Income
LE Lifestyle Expenses
LA Liquid Assets

Freedom is when Leveraged Income covers your Lifestyle Expenses, backed by enough Liquid Assets to cover any gaps

The Three Freedoms

01

Money Freedom

Your assets and leveraged income cover your lifestyle, so work becomes a choice rather than a requirement

02

Mobility Freedom

Income that isn’t tied to a location, so you can live and work wherever you want

03

Mind Freedom

The clarity and peace that come from being in control—the freedom that matters most
